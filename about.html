About Daiko Industrial Solutions

Pioneering the convergence of construction expertise and cloud technology since 2020.

Our Journey

Founded in March 2020

Based in Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh, Daiko Industrial Solutions emerged from a vision to bridge the gap between traditional construction practices and modern technology. Our founders, Uttam Kumar Samal and Lopamudra Barma, recognized that the Indian construction sector — valued at over $700 billion — still relied heavily on manual processes, paper-based documentation, and fragmented communication channels.

DPIIT Recognition

From day one, we set out to build solutions that construction professionals actually need. Starting with civil engineering consulting services, we quickly expanded into IoT-enabled site monitoring and cloud-based project management tools. Our DPIIT recognition as a startup validated our innovative approach to solving real industry challenges.

Expansion & Growth

Today, we serve clients across Uttar Pradesh, Delhi NCR, and Rajasthan, helping them modernize their operations, reduce project overruns, and ensure regulatory compliance through technology-first solutions.

Our Vision

"To become India's leading construction technology company, making every construction site smarter, safer, and more efficient through cloud-native solutions."

Our Mission

"To empower construction firms with accessible, affordable technology that transforms how projects are planned, executed, and monitored — reducing waste, improving safety, and accelerating delivery."
