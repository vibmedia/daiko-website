Who We Are

Building Tomorrow's Infrastructure, Today

Founded in 2020 and headquartered in Ghaziabad, Daiko Industrial Solutions is an innovative technology and engineering firm dedicated to transforming the Indian construction industry. We bridge the gap between heavy civil engineering and advanced cloud-native solutions.
