Our Services

End-to-end construction and industrial technology solutions.

Smart Site Monitoring

Our IoT-powered monitoring platform connects sensors across your construction sites to provide real-time visibility into equipment health, worker safety compliance, and environmental conditions. Automated alerts notify project managers of potential issues before they become costly problems.

  • Real-time equipment health tracking via IoT sensors
  • Worker safety compliance monitoring (PPE detection)
  • Environmental monitoring (dust, noise, temperature)
  • Automated alert system with SMS and email notifications
  • Historical data analytics and trend reporting

Cloud-Based Project Management

Our centralized platform enables architects, engineers, and project managers to collaborate in real-time on Building Information Modeling (BIM) files, project schedules, and regulatory documentation. Built on AWS infrastructure for reliability and security.

  • BIM file collaboration and version control
  • Gantt chart scheduling with milestone tracking
  • Document management with regulatory compliance tagging
  • Multi-site dashboard for portfolio-level visibility
  • Role-based access control for stakeholders
